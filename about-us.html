About Paathshala

We are a premiere education institute and one of the fastest growing education institutes in India. Founded and managed by IITians, Doctors and Educationists, we are focused on providing quality education with state-of-the-art infrastructure, like nowhere in India and guidance for the students of XI and XII standard science, aspiring to crack competitive entrance exams like the IIT JEE, BITSAT, NEET, JIPMER, AIIMS etc and Boards

Our primary aim is of delivering quality education to aspiring Engineers, Doctors, Scientists, who will go on to become successful professionals. Since the inception, we as a team of professional and experienced teachers have always aimed to deliver higher level quality coaching for students studying at School (10th Std.), Junior College (11th and 12th Science) and across the years of professional courses like Engineering.
